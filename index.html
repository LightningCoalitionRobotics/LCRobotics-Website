ABOUT US

The Lightning Coalition robotics team is made up of a group of students from the Annapolis area, that want to learn and develop themselves through the experiences offered through FIRST. Our team was founded by many former members of the disbanded Zagbots #7594. While the Zagbots team was disbanded only after its rookie year, a majority of the members were still driven to participate in FIRST. This drive, to satisfy our innate curiosity and desire to learn as people, led us to start Lightning Coalition Robotics. Our goals are to enhance and develop our skills through continued participation of FIRSTâ€™s FTC. As a team, we wish to satisfy our curiosity through encountering new challenges and fulfil our desire to learn through working together to overcoming these challenges. It is our core belief that by connecting with FIRST and competing in FIRSTâ€™s FTC, each member of our team, Lightning Coalition Robotics, will leave with useful life experiences that will enrich their lives.

SPONSERSHIP

Because we are an independent team, there is no higer organization whatsoever, leaving us to figure out how to pay for ourselves. As a base plan, we split the costs of the team evenly among the members, although this is really not ideal. We seek out sponsors so that the team can run at a lower cost for its members. For sponsoring us, you get a spot on our sponsors section seen on our website. You also get a sticker on our robot for competition. We also hope that it will turn into a relationship between our team and you in any way fit, such as hosting outreach events together. Whether it is sponsorship in terms of raw materials, mentorships, or monetary funds, we are always greatful for any help we recieve. If you are interested in helping out our team, please contact us!
