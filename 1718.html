Challenge Name: Relic Recovery
Team Member Count: 10
Year: 2017 - 2018



Goal

The new season has begun! This season has brought a completely new challenge. This year, the challenge is called Relic Recovery. It consists of three parts. The first is autonomous, second is tele-op, then third is the endgame. The aim of the game is to place the relic onto one of the scoring pads. You can get to the point to be able to do this through many ways. The easiest of these is to fill up a rack with cubes.
